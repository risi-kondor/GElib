*******************
SO3bivec/SObivecArr
*******************

``SO3bivec`` and ``SO3bivecArr`` are the bi-covariant versions of the ``SO3vec`` and ``SO3vecArr`` classes. 
An ``SO3bivec`` object can either be initialized from (a) a sequence of ``SO3bipart`` objects 
corresponding to each of its parts; (b) a sequence of single precision complext ``torch.Tensor`` 
objects of the appropriate sizes or (c) via the ``zeros`` or ``randn`` constructors. 
In the last of these three cases, the constructor expects a list of ``[l1,l2,n]`` triples, 
for example:

.. code-block:: python

 >>> u=gelib.SO3bivec.randn(1,[[1,2,1],[2,2,3]])
 >>> u
 <GElib::SO3bivec b=1, tau=[((2,2):3)((1,2):1)]>

of each component of the ``CGtransform`` of each component of the ``SO3bivec``:

 >>> v=gelib.CGtransform(u)
 >>> v
 <GElib::SO3vec b=1, tau=(3,4,4,4,3)>
